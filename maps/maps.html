My Little Palantir!

If you happen to have some photos on your computer which were captured on a GPS enabled device, such as your smart phone or tablet, you can visualize them on a map.

PS: If an image doesn't have the GPS information, it might appear somewhere else!

(Disclaimer: Your photos never leave your computer. They are totally private and safe.)

Sorry, this application does not support your current screen resolution. Please rotate your Phone to Landscape orientation.
